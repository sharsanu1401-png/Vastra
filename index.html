Our Story

Where Tradition Meets
Modern Fashion

Vastra blends tradition with contemporary fashion, creating pieces that honour the rich textile heritage of India while embracing global design sensibilities. Each garment is thoughtfully crafted to celebrate the intersection of culture, craftsmanship, and couture.

Founded with a passion for sustainable luxury, every Vastra piece is made with ethically sourced fabrics and produced by artisan communities across the country.
